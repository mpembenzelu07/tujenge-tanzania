How Our Marketplace Works

Supporting Tanzanian producers while getting amazing products

1

Farmers & Artisans List Products

Local producers upload their products with fair prices set by our pricing algorithm.

2

You Browse & Purchase

Customers worldwide browse our verified products and make purchases securely.

3

We Handle Logistics

Our team manages quality control, packaging, and international shipping.

4

Producers Get Paid Fairly

Farmers receive 70% of the sale price (vs. 20-30% in traditional supply chains).
