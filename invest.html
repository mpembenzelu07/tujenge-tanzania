Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about investing with us

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment varies by opportunity, ranging from $500 to $2,500. We're committed to making impact investing accessible to both retail and institutional investors.

How are returns paid to investors?

Returns are typically paid quarterly or semi-annually, depending on the project. You can choose to receive dividends or reinvest them automatically for compound growth.

What is the typical investment term?

Investment terms range from 3 to 7 years, with most opportunities structured as 5-year investments. We provide clear exit strategies for each opportunity.

How do you measure social impact?

We use the Impact Management Project framework to track metrics across five dimensions: What, Who, How Much, Contribution, and Risk. Investors receive quarterly impact reports alongside financial statements.

Is my investment secured by assets?

Yes, most of our investments are secured by tangible assets such as solar equipment, agricultural infrastructure, or real estate. Each opportunity document specifies the security arrangements.
