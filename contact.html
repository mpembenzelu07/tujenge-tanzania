Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How can I invest in Tujenge Tanzania projects?

You can invest through our investment portal on the Invest page. We offer various investment opportunities with different risk profiles and returns. Minimum investment starts at $500. Contact our Investment Director Paul William for personalized guidance.

How do I become a seller on your marketplace?

Visit our Marketplace page and click on "Become a Seller." You'll need to register your business, provide necessary documentation, and our team will verify your products. We support farmers, artisans, and small businesses across Tanzania.

What is your response time for inquiries?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Urgent matters are prioritized. For investment-related questions, we aim to respond within 4 hours during business hours.

Do you offer partnership opportunities for NGOs?

Yes, we actively partner with NGOs, government agencies, and international organizations. Contact our Community Development Head Blessing George Mtangoo to discuss potential collaborations and partnership models.

How can I volunteer or intern with Tujenge Tanzania?

We welcome volunteers and interns in various departments. Send your CV and motivation letter to careers@tujenge-tz.co.tz. Please specify your area of interest and availability.
