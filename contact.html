Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Investment-related queries are prioritized and typically receive responses within 4 hours.

What are your business hours?

Our offices are open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM East Africa Time. We're also available by appointment outside these hours for important matters.

Can I schedule a meeting with your team?

Yes, you can schedule meetings through our contact form. Please indicate your preferred date and time, and our team will confirm availability.

Do you have regional offices?

Yes, we have regional offices in Arusha, Mwanza, and Zanzibar. Contact our main office to be connected with the regional team nearest to you.
